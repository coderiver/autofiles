Acura vehicles

Acura, Japanese automobile brand, was established in March 1986 as the luxury vehicle division of Honda Motor Company to enter competition with BMW, Lexus and Mercedes-Benz in the luxury cars market.

Acura was first unveiled in the USA and Canada in 1986 and later to the rest of the world. Around fifteen thousand cars are sold under Acura brand in the USA each month, ranking the luxury-manufacturer 21 in the automakers sales reports with market share of 1%.
